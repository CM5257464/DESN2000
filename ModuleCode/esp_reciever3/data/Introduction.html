Scenario

Global temperatures are increasing. Sea levels are rising. Species are going extinct. And we need your help to solve the climate crisis. Welcome to Energyville, a small town with a population of 1500 people located on the outskirts of Sydney, characterised by low to moderate winds year-round and sunny summers. Currently, the energy outlook is:

  • Annual demand of 120 MWh supplied by a coal fired power station
  • Annual cost of $75k
  • Releases enough CO2 to fill 10 Olympic swimming pools

As an energy expert, you are being called upon to transition the town’s current energy generation to the highest contribution of renewable energy possible. To achieve this, you have 5 portions you can spend across the three renewable energy sources, but only maximum of 3 on any one option. You must use all portions by and choose wisely because there is no going back!
